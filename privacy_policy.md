# Privacy Policy for ClassHub

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

ClassHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in our School Management application.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local-Only Storage
ClassHub operates as an **offline-first application**. All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.

### Types of Data Stored Locally
- **Student Information**: Names, student IDs, class assignments, parent contact information, and optional photos
- **Attendance Records**: Check-in/check-out times, attendance status, and notes
- **Class Information**: Class names, descriptions, and teacher assignments
- **Announcements**: Teacher announcements with optional photo attachments
- **Messages**: Parent-teacher messages stored locally
- **Teacher Information**: Names, email addresses, and role assignments

### Photo Storage
- Photos are stored locally on your device
- Camera permission is requested **only when you explicitly choose to add a photo**
- Photos are not uploaded to any external service

## Permissions

### Camera Permission
- **When Requested**: Only when you tap the "Add Photo" button
- **Purpose**: To allow you to attach photos to student profiles, announcements, or messages
- **Storage**: Photos are stored locally on your device only

### Storage Permission (Android)
- **When Requested**: Only when exporting data via Storage Access Framework (SAF)
- **Purpose**: To save exported reports to your chosen location
- **Scope**: Limited to the specific file you choose to save

## Data Export and Import

### Export Functionality
- You can export all data to JSON format using Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF)
- Exports are saved to a location of your choice
- No data is transmitted during export

### Import Functionality
- You can import previously exported data
- Imported data replaces existing local data
- All import operations are performed locally

## Third-Party Services

ClassHub does not integrate with any third-party services, cloud storage, or analytics platforms. All functionality operates entirely on your device.

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted at rest using Android's built-in encryption
- No network transmission of personal data
- No data sharing with third parties

## Children's Privacy

ClassHub is designed for use by teachers and administrators. While the app may contain information about students (including minors), this information:
- Is stored only on the device of the authorized user
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Can be deleted at any time by the user

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Remove any data through the app's interface
- **Export**: Export all data for backup purposes
- **Control**: Full control over when permissions are requested

## Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you:
- Delete it through the app interface
- Uninstall the app (which removes all local data)
- Export and then clear the database

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and this document.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's settings screen.

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- Google Play Console Privacy Policy requirements (2025-2026)
- Android's permission model and best practices
- Data minimization principles (only collects what is necessary)
- User consent requirements (permissions requested only when needed)

---

**Note**: This is a local-only application. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
